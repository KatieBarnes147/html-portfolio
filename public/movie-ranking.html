Great Millenial Movies According to Katie

These are just some of my favs.

1. American Pie

The original American Pie movie's (4 of them) were so funny and completely inappropriate (should not be a teen movie but was back then lol)

2. Gladiator

This movie was suspenseful, exciting and very unpredictable (gore and violence).

3. Mean Girls

This movie was literally how High school was in the 90's you had to pick a crowd (bullying, and toxic behavior).

4. Harold and Kumar

Harold and Kumar has 3 movies all of which were funny as heck. Another teen movie that is crazy funny but completely inappropriate.

5. 10 Things I hate about you

Another teen movie based on the pressures of being a teenager and peer pressure. This movie was full of emotions both happy and sad.

6. My Girl

My Girl was a great movie it was very emotional and happiness turned into grief. It would be my first experience as a child and feeling a sense of loss but in a movie of course.

7. The Notebook

If you ever been in love you deeply feel the emotions of this movie. Such a great love story!

8. The Lion King

I would have to say this movie right here.. this is every millenial child's favorite animated movie! Full of joy and music and emotions.

9. Scream

Even is this thriller is not in your top 10 I can guarantee as millenial you went to the theater to see this with your friends! The mask if the scream serial killer is still a halloween costume of choice today! Every teen back then was like " Wazzzzzuuuuuuppppppppp" lol.

10. Titanic

Come on this movie was great back then! Good love story, suspenseful.
